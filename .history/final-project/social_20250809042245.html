Social Growth

Building strong connections and uplifting communities through service, kindness, and shared experiences.

Service Opportunities

Serving others strengthens relationships and brings joy. Explore ways to help your community:

  • Organize a neighborhood clean-up.
  • Visit a local care home and spend time with residents.
  • Participate in a food drive or clothing donation.

Friendship and Fellowship

Build lasting friendships by engaging in wholesome activities together:

  • Host a game night or potluck dinner.
  • Plan group hikes or outdoor activities.
  • Join a community choir, sports team, or hobby club.

Church Activities

Participate in activities organized by The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints to meet new people and strengthen bonds.
